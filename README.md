## 🚀 ResQSense – Invisible Victim Detection System

**ResQSense** is an AI-powered disaster management platform designed to detect and prioritize 
“invisible victims” — people who cannot call for help during emergencies.

The system analyzes signals like inactivity, last-known location, and environmental data to identify high-risk 
“silent zones” where victims may be trapped. It then intelligently allocates rescue teams and resources based on
severity and survival probability.

### 🌟 Key Features
* 🔴 **Silent Zone Detection** – Identifies areas with no activity but high risk
* 🗺️ **Live Disaster Dashboard** – Real-time map with severity levels
* ⚙️ **Smart Resource Allocation** – Optimized rescue and supply distribution
* 🤝 **Volunteer Network** – Connects nearby helpers in real time
* 📡 **Offline Support** – Emergency alerts via SMS

### 🎯 Goal
To ensure faster, fairer, and more effective disaster response by helping rescuers find those who cannot ask for help.
